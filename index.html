About Julian

Julian Harms writes dystopian and dark fantasy fiction where survival comes at a cost and hope refuses to die.

Her stories explore fractured worlds, inherited fear, and the quiet defiance of those who refuse to disappear. In worlds shaped by collapse and consequence, survival is never the end of the story; it is just the beginning.

Julian lives in the American West where she builds worlds out of time.
